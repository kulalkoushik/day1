About Onam

Onam is an annual Hindu festival celebrated in Kerala, India. It is a harvest festival that marks the homecoming of King Mahabali and is celebrated with joy, feasts, and traditional dances.

Pookalam

The beautiful floral decoration made during Onam to welcome King Mahabali.

Vallam Kali

Also known as the snake boat race, an exciting part of the Onam celebration.

Onam Sadya

A grand feast served on banana leaves, featuring an array of traditional Kerala dishes.
